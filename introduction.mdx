## What is PayOnProof?

PayOnProof (POP) is a simple interface that aggregates multiple Stellar anchors and routes cross-border payments through the best available rate in real-time. It's not a bank or marketplace—it's the layer that optimizes how SMEs pay internationally.

## The problem

SMEs and freelancers in LATAM lose between **3% and 8% per international payment** due to:

- Hidden exchange rate spreads
- Accumulated correspondent bank fees
- Unpredictable settlement times (3-5 business days)
- Lack of transparency about real costs

While Stellar anchors exist with better conditions, integrating them individually is technically complex for the average SME.

**Result:**
- Eroded margins
- Reduced competitiveness
- Constant operational friction

## The solution

PayOnProof aggregates multiple Stellar anchors into a single interface and:

- Queries rates in real-time
- Normalizes fees and conditions
- Allows manual selection or "Choose for me" auto-routing
- Executes payment through the most economical route
- Records a verifiable on-chain event

**What you see:**

```
Amount sent → Amount received → Total fee → Estimated time
```

No complex wallets. No technical integrations. No opacity.

## How PayOnProof works (4 steps)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Select your origin country, destination country, and amount. PayOnProof instantly queries all available anchor routes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare routes">
    View side-by-side comparison of fees, settlement speed, escrow options, and anchor operational status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute transfer">
    One click to execute. Optional escrow protection secures funds through every leg of the journey.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive proof">
    A verifiable, portable Proof of Payment is generated on-chain. Share it with anyone, anywhere.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PayOnProof does not custody funds long-term. KYC and compliance are handled by the anchors themselves.
</Note>

## Stellar standards used

PayOnProof is built on established Stellar Ecosystem Proposals (SEPs):

- **SEP-1**: Anchor information discovery via `stellar.toml`
- **SEP-10**: Authentication via challenge/response flow
- **SEP-24**: Interactive anchor transfers (deposit/withdraw)
- **SEP-31**: Cross-border payments direct to anchors

## On-chain vs off-chain

### On-chain (Stellar blockchain)

- Transaction hash
- Anchor used
- Timestamp
- Final settled amount
- Verifiable event ID

### Off-chain (PayOnProof API)

- Route simulations
- Personal data
- User preferences
- Savings analytics

PayOnProof does not custody funds. All transactions settle through Stellar anchors with established compliance processes.
